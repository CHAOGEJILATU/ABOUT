## GLOBAL PROFILE

**CHAOGEJILATU** IS AN ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENTIST BASED IN JAPAN, WORKING ACROSS THE FIELDS OF **CLIMATE CHANGE**, **LAND-SYSTEM SCIENCE**, **AGRICULTURE & FOOD SECURITY**, AND **DATA-DRIVEN ENVIRONMENTAL ASSESSMENT**.

THIS PROFILE HIGHLIGHTS A RESEARCH IDENTITY THAT COMBINES:

- **EARTH OBSERVATION**
- **GEOSPATIAL & STATISTICAL ANALYSIS**
- **REPRODUCIBLE SCIENTIFIC WORKFLOWS**
- **INTERNATIONAL VISIBILITY THROUGH GLOBAL AUTHORITY-CONTROL AND KNOWLEDGE SYSTEMS**
